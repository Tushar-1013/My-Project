Printable clothing banners by Canva

When there’s a new event at your store, it’s best to have both online and offline marketing materials to help spread the news. Offline marketing materials, in particular, can help pique the curiosity of passersby and entice them to walk through your store’s entrance. So the next time you’re opening a new branch, having a sale, or offering a new line of seasonal clothing apparel, go all out with outdoor advertising. Proudly display a customized clothing banner template made on Canva.

Browse through our selection of professionally crafted designs for all your clothing banner needs. We have a wide assortment of banner formats, styles, themes, and colors. Whether you specialize in high-end brands or pet costumes, you’re bound to find a cloth store banner design you can customize further.

Dress up your chosen template in just a few minutes for free via our app or on our website. Using our beginner-friendly editing tools, simply drag and drop graphic elements from our extensive library to tweak your layout. Change the fonts, illustrations, or even the preset colors of the clothes banner design.

Keep the text short but catchy. Include important shop details such as your name, the promo being offered, and its duration period. Then, upload the best product photos to match your copy. All these visual cues can serve as the push people need to head into your establishment.

Remember, there’s no free trial time limit on Canva, so you can keep making changes to the banner design for your cloth shop until you’re satisfied. Ready to have your work printed? Download it in print-ready file format, and send it to your local printing shop.

Or why not avail of Canva Print? We produce high-quality banners on eco-friendly materials in the size and quantity you need. Plus, we’ll deliver your printed banner designs and on time, so you can display them outside your clothes shop as you launch your latest promotion.
